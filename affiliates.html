How are the estimated earnings calculated?

To ensure a fair and realistic estimate of your potential earnings, we calculate them considering both the growth from new referrals and the natural customer lifecycle, including a 20% monthly churn rate. Here's how it works:

Each month, your earnings are initially based on the total number of users you refer. However, we understand that not all users stay indefinitely. To represent this realistically, we apply a 20% churn rate, which means we assume that each month, about 20% of the existing referred users might stop using the service.

By accounting for this, we provide a more accurate and fair estimate of what you can expect to earn. The initial growth from new users combined with the adjustment for churn gives a balanced view of your ongoing commissions. This method helps both new and seasoned affiliates set realistic expectations and understand their potential earnings over time.
